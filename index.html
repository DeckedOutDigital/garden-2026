💧 Irrigation Guide

⏱
Summer Schedule
Run drip 20–30 min daily in peak heat (Jul–Aug)
🍃
Spring/Fall Schedule
Run drip every other day in moderate temps
🪴
Mulch Saves Water
2–3" straw/wood chips reduces needs by 30–50%
🫙
Containers
Check daily — they dry faster than beds. Hand-water as needed.
☀
Best Time
Morning watering reduces disease pressure
Single zone note: All beds and shade sections share one drip zone. Schedule must balance the thirstiest plants with those that prefer less water.
